Lead a Session

Sessions are 40 minutes, followed by 10 minutes of questions (for a total of 50 minutes). To lead a session, send us an email with the following information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Department
  • Job Title
  • Session Title
  • Session Description
  • Target Audience
  • Level
  • Language

Topics

Based on the feedback we have received from CodeFest participants in previous years, we have decided to divide topics into subject matters that will cater to each audience.

All of these topics will provide participants with an opportunity to learn or share knowledge, as well as bring it back to apply it to their daily jobs.

Implementers

  • How to use WET plug-ins
  • WET Tips and Tricks
  • Accessibility and WET
  • Adobe Experience Manager (AEM)
  • Application Implementation

Contributors

  • Building for WET
  • Build a WET Theme
  • Build a WET Plug-in
  • Agile Development for Developers
  • Grunt & Nodes
  • Future of WET

Experts

  • HTML 5 and Beyond
  • JavaScript 1.6
  • Accessible Rich Internet Applications (WAI-ARIA)
  • Usability UX (Web)

Project Managers

  • Agile Project Management
  • WET Enhancements
  • Accessibility Risk Management
  • Web Accessibility in Contracts

Writers

  • Writing for Mobile
  • Accessibility in Microsoft Word Documents
  • Understanding Web Accessibility
